------------------------------------
Modular Lowpoly Track Free
------------------------------------

	Modular Lowpoly Track Free contains 8 meshes and 8 prefabs with 1 track.

	Features:

		• 3 bridges modules.
		• 2 corners modules.
		• 3 fences modules.
		• 2 straight modules of the road.
		• 1 start/finish.
		• One texture 64×64 for all meshes.

	Compatible:

		• Unity 2018.4.0f1 or higher.
